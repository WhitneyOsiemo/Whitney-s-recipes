  • Lasagna

    • Description

    Lasagna is a classic Italian dish loved worldwide.Layered with wide pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a medley of cheese, it delivers a satisfying blend of flavors.The interplay between tender pasta, savory meat or vegetables, and melted cheese creates a comforting and hearty culinary experience that never disappoints.

    Ingredients

    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 ounces or 400g) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 ounces or 170g) tomato paste
    • 1 can (8 ounces or 227g) tomato sauce
    • 2 teaspoons dried basil
    • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
    • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent and the beef is fully cooked.
    4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine. Simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, and chopped parsley (if using). Mix well.
      6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top. Spread about half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
    6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden.
    7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the lasagna to set
  • Risotto

    • Description

    Risotto, a creamy Italian rice dish, tantalizes taste buds with its rich and comforting flavors. Its velvety texture, infused with aromatic herbs and complemented by various ingredients, creates a culinary masterpiece.

    Ingredients

    • 1 ½ cups Arborio rice
    • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • ½ cup dry white wine
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh herbs (such as thyme or parsley) for garnish (optional)

    Steps

    1. In a large saucepan, heat the broth and keep it simmering over low heat.
    2. In a separate large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become translucent.
    3. Add the Arborio rice to the pot and stir it for about a minute until the grains are coated with butter and slightly toasted.
    4. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice.
    5. Begin adding the simmering broth, one ladleful at a time, stirring continuously. Allow the rice to absorb the broth before adding more. Continue this process until the rice is al dente, tender with a slight firmness.
    6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue stirring until the cheese is melted and the risotto becomes creamy.
    7. Remove the pot from heat and let it rest for a few minutes.
    8. Serve the risotto hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
  • pasta

    • Description

    Pasta, a versatile Italian staple, satisfies cravings with its comforting texture and endless possibilities. From classic spaghetti to vibrant lasagna, it brings people together, celebrating the art of simple yet delicious meals.

    Ingredients

    • 8 ounces of pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)
    • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

    Steps

    1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
    3. Add the diced tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend together.
    4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it in the sauce until well coated. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the pasta.
    5. Remove from heat and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil or parsley, if desired.
    6. Serve hot and enjoy your easy and flavorful pasta dish.
